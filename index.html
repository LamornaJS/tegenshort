Tegen Short, born 16 June 1996, is a Guildhall Drama student in her third year. Tegen has performed several roles while at Guildhall including Cordelia in King Lear, Helen in The Trojan Women, and Beth in Merrily We Roll Along. Most recently she wrote, directed and stared in her own performance.

Prior to Guildhall, Tegen had some professional experience, playing Gemma in Murder, an ITV television series, playing Ellie in Being Nice, a short film, and performing in two music videos for Mount Kimbie and Joy Crookes.

Tegen is a strong singer (mezzo-soprano), Grade 8 Trinity Guildhall, has experience with 20th century dance, show dance, stage combat and basic acrobatics. She also has a passion for sport, having played County cricket for nine year, football to a high level, and a keen horse rider.
